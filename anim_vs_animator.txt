## Key Visual Rules for the Agent

**Impact starbursts:** 6 lines, each 3px long, radiating outward from a center point at 60-degree intervals. Visible for exactly 2 frames then erase completely.

**Bounce physics:** When the figure lands after a fall, the hip drops 3px below the landing y-coordinate over 2 frames, then returns to the landing y-coordinate over 2 frames. No further oscillation.

**Running cycle:** Two poses alternating every 2 frames. Pose A: left leg line goes from hip to (hipX-3, hipY+5), right leg line goes from hip to (hipX+3, hipY+3). Right arm swings forward. Pose B: mirrored. Arms always counter-swing opposite to legs.

**Walking cycle:** Same as running but poses alternate every 3 frames and hip advances 2px per frame instead of 3px.

**Screen flash:** Invert all pixels for exactly 1 frame. Do not sustain beyond that frame.

**Cursor erasing a line:** The 6x6px eraser rectangle moves across the line. Any pixel within the eraser bounds is set to black each frame. The erasure is the intersection of the eraser rect and the existing drawn pixels.

**Stretch effect:** When the figure is dragged beyond 6 frames of sustained drag, increase the horizontal pixel distance between the head center and the hip anchor by 1px per additional frame. Cap at 8px total stretch. All limb endpoints scale proportionally outward from the spine axis. The spine itself elongates horizontally. Lines remain 1px wide regardless of stretch.

**Cursor path between scenes:** The cursor travels in a straight line between its last confirmed position and its next required position unless the script describes an arc. Straight-line travel uses linear interpolation across the minimum required frames at approximately 4–6px per frame. No cursor position is ever hardcoded at the start of a scene without first verifying it matches the prior scene's final cursor position.

**Figure position between scenes:** The figure's hip position is a continuously tracked variable. Any gap between a scene's required start position and the prior scene's final hip position must be filled with walking frames at 2px per frame before the new scene's action begins.
